Heroic - Grong

This is a single target encounter with a single add that spawns at ~20s and then every minute thereafter. Your usual highest simming single target setup with Demonic Consumption will generally work best here although Nether Portal remains a viable option. Demo is somewhat lackluster at killing the adds but should not hinder the raid unless add damage is extremely low overall. Damage intake on this fight is quite high so Demo's tankiness from Soul Link is extremely useful. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • You can pool Demonic Core charges before add spawns to allow for some additional burst.
  • Movement is generally low, but Soul Strike is a good option depending on your range stack strategy and is more forgiving to play than Vilefiend as it adds an instant cast.
  • Dark Pact almost perfectly aligns with the Tantrum / Death Knell and can help alleviate a lot of the healing, Demon Skin provides more overall absorbtion if Trantrums / Death Knells aren't causing issues.
  • Demonic Circle can be used to quickly get out of fire puddles or get back from running the throw out of the group.

    • Mythic - Grong

    The mythic version of this encounter is essentially the same as heroic except 2 identical adds spawn every 2 minutes rather than 1 add every 1 minute. The standard 5-6min kill time is quite poor for Nether Portal so Demonic Consumption is by far the preferred choice. The damage is also significantly higher and the fight is frequently 6 healed to alleviate incoming damage so the above statements about defensive traits and talents are more applicable in mythic.

  • The two adds usually spawn in close proximity so switch to Implosion rotation when both are up.
  • The fight is typically under 6 mins with lust at 40%, so you will only get 2 uses of Nether Portal. Typically you will save the 2nd portal and 3rd Tyrant for lust, but check your kill times to make sure you are not wasting tyrant casts.
