Heroic - Jadefire

This is a two target stacked cleave encounter with some add spawns and you will spend most of the fight focusing Mestrah / Ma'ra to prevent her final phase. This fight is a bit more movement intesive than the prior fights plus has some target swapping so Soul Strike has some additional utility. None of the 100 talents add anything to cleave so either of the recommended options are strong. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Pool cores to maximize damage on the living bombs and tiger adds, but otherwise focus on burning Mestrah / Ma'ra.
  • Shadowfury can be used to stun the tiger adds.
  • Circle can be placed near a living bomb location (they are fixed) to allow for faster swaps.
  • You can gateway over significant portions of the maze without getting knocked back, see the below image for placement.

    • Mythic - Jadefire

    The mythic version of this encounter is identical to heroic except that you are nearly guarenteed to be ported up to the Multi-Sided Strike and thus spend a decent amount of time unable to attack the boss and you recieve a damage buff upon completing the minigame. Since the first Multi-Sided Strike happens around 30s into the pull, Nether Portal is not recommended in favor of the shorter burst of Demonic Consumption.

  • All adds have more health in mythic so hard swapping with more dots and UAs than heroic is likely necessary.
  • The order for the Multi-Sided Strike is always Blue -> Green -> Red -> Green -> Blue.
