Heroic - Jaina Proudmoore

This is a multi-phased primarily single target encounter with some priority add burns. Phase 1 is pretty much entirely single target as adds are either Mind Controlled off the boat or obliterated before you can deal any significant damage to them. Phase 2 has more targets, namely ice blocks and ordinances that must be burned. Phase 3 is 3 separate burst adds (Ice Wall, Nathanos, Tidewater Elemental). Phase 4 is a single target burn with ranged priority adds (Prismatic Images), and ice blocks. Since most adds will not live long enough for Agony to ramp, Siphon Life is the go-to talent. Inevitable Demise is useful for quickly bursting ordinances, ice wall, tide elemental, and prismatic images, otherwse normal single-target traits take priority. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Unexploded Ordnance barrels can be used to generate shards and ID stacks with Agony, make sure to watch damage on them and stop dots if needed as early breaks can cause wipes.
  • Imp can dispel the Grasp of Frost and is also the best pet for swapping targets.
  • Gateway can be quite useful in p1 to clear fire on far ballistas, and in p2 and p3 to reposition around icy patches and get to ordnances and images.
