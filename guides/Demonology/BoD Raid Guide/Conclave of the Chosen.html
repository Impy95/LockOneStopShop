Heroic - Conclave of the Chosen

This is a single target priority burn encounter with some add spawns. There are always 2 bosses present until the end of the encounter however the second boss heals to full when the first dies. This fight has pretty high movement and a lot of target swaps so Demonic Strength and Soul Strike are generally preferred although the other usual options remain strong. Demonic Consumption is superior for this encounter as the movement required makes Nether Portal fairly impractical. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Pool cores for raptor spawns to maximize damage on them, Demo will not have the best damage on these but they are still the highest priority target while they are active so do the best you can.
  • Shadowfury and Axe Toss can be used to stun the raptor adds.
  • Gateway can be used to move across the room to Pa'ku's Wrath positions, 2 gateways will cover all possible spawns.
  • Circle should be placed in the middle of the room to reset after Paku and run from raptors.

    • Mythic - Conclave of the Chosen
