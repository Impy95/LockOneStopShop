Heroic - Stormwall Blockade

This is a multi-phased encounter with a mix of single target and spread cleave. Phase 1 is largely a single target burn of the two phase one bosses while ocasionally dealing with a Siren while Brother Joseph is on your boat. Phase 2 is a spread cleave phase with adds spawning at range that must be slowed and bursted down. Any of your usual talent setups with Demonic Consumption will work well here as you simply cannot make ample use of Nether Portal due to phase timing and frequent movement, As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Pool cores to help burst sirens and storm elementals.
  • Burning Rush can be useful for clearing more Freezing Tidepools when afflicted with Storm's Wail.
  • Circle can be used to get back to the ship in phase 1 if you fail to damage the siren before being thrown off.
  • Gateway can be placed either to move across the platform to dodge Sea Swells and soak Ire's of the Deep.

    • Mythic - Stormwall Blockade

    The major difference in the mythic version of this encounter is that the phase 1 mechanics continue into phase 2, namely the siren spawns. This turns a primarily single-target burn into a heavily multi-target phase. Swap to adds as needed, but since demo has little multi-dot capability this should still be treated as a largely single target encounter with some add swaps as needed.
