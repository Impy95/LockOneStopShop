Heroic - High Tinker Mekkatorque

This is a single target encounter with some damage immune adds. None of the usual selection of talents are particularly favored and each have their usual advantages so any combination of the recommended talents will work well. Since incoming damage is quite high and movement is minor throughout the encounter, either defensive talent will be more valuable than Burning Rush. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Gateway and Circle are both very useful for running Gigavolt Charges behind walls and returning to the group.

Mythic - High Tinker Mekkatorque

The Mythic version of this encounter is basically the same as Heroic except the spark bots reflect damage via Spark Shield and Wormhole Generator can knock you up or Polymorph you in addition to the normal teleport. Defensive talents are highly recommended for progress. The phase timing in Mythic is quite poor for Nether Portal so Demonic Consumption is highly preferred as you get 3 Tyrants in Phase 1 and 2 in Phase 2 while you only get 1 Nether Portal per phase.

  • Demonic Circle should be used exclusively for Wormhole Generator in mythic as negating the fall damage is a huge boon.
