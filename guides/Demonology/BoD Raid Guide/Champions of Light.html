Heroic - Champions of Light

This is a fairly straightforward single target encounter with some spread adds. The adds that spawn will heal each other so are typically tanked together away from the boss. For Demonology, this encounter is just a single target burn as you gain no additional resources from hitting the adds unless you take Doom, so your standard single target low-movement setup will do nicely. Nether Portal works well here as the fight is short, Bloodlust/Heroism is on pull, and there is very little movement. However, Demonic Consumption remains a strong choice. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • If you take Doom you can multi-dot the adds for some additional shards. Make sure you have a good way to track your Dooms or you'll overflow shards and waste the talent.
  • Make sure to save Bilescourge Bombers and a decent pack of imps to implode on the add swap.
  • Face away from the crusader whenever he casts his ability or you will get disoriented.

Mythic - Champions of Light

The Mythic version of this encounter is basically the same as Heroic with two simple changes; one of the adds throws hammers at the raid that must be dodged, and the healing adds cast Angelic Renewal that must be interrupted or the boss will heal to full.

  • Demonology has no interrupt and the adds are stun immune so you cannot assist with interrupts.

    • Available Loot

    • Bracers: Bracers of Zealous Calling
    • Chest: Divine Fury Raiment
    • Dagger: Desecrated Blade of the Disciples
