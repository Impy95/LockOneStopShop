Stat priorities are fairly straight forward and don't require much (or any) thought, the best way to look at getting stats is as follows: Any scenario:

  • Intellect
  • Haste
  • Critical Strike
  • Mastery
  • Versatility

Generally speaking something with Haste and either Critical Strike or Mastery will be best.

Intellect

This is our primary stat, everything we do scales with it.

Haste

Haste rating increases the speed we cast, the rate our DoT ticks, the cast rate and attack speed of all your Demons; including your primary pet (Felguard), Wild Imps (summoned through Hand of Gul'dan), Dreadstalkers and Doomguard/Infernal. You will need 68 rating for 1% haste.

Mastery

Provides a damage % gain for our demons. In BFA each spec has a different modifier for the amount of rating needed per percent gain. For Demonology a 1% damage increase is 72 Mastery Rating. If you want to see the formula it's here:
((SpecModifier*Rating)/72)/100
So to find 1% for Demonology:
72/1.45=49.66
Which makes the calculation:
((1.45*49.66)/72)/100 = 0.01 which in game translates to 1%

Critical Strike

Increases your critical strike chance by 1% every 72 rating.

Versatility

Versatility is purposefully designed to be a flat DPS gain, it increases damage and healing and reduces damage taken by a flat %. To gain 1% versatility you need 85 rating.

Azerite Traits

To view all information about relics please visit the Sims > Azerite page here.
As always Blizzard constantly tunes Azerite traits in an attempt to have them all be mostly equal. Right now there are a few stand out traits for Demonology:

  • Supreme Commander
  • Baleful Invocation
  • Shadow's Bite
  • Demonic Meteor - in particular for AoE situations, like M+
  • Archive of the Titans
There are additional traits that are serviceable for pure single target:
  • Thunderous Blast
  • Rezan's Fury
  • Tidal Surge
  • Filthy Transfusion
  • Dagger in the Back - requires you to be behind your target at all times to be good!
Furthermore these are other traits you can target but are not as optimal:
  • Blightborne Infusion
  • Tradewinds
  • Swirling Sands
  • Incite the Pack
  • Meticulous Scheming
  • Sylvanas' Resolve
  • Champion of Azeroth

Gems, Enchants and Consumables


Rings: Enchant Ring - Pact of Haste or the cheaper version Enchant Ring - Seal of Haste
Weapon: Enchant Weapon - Quick Navigation
Flask: Flask of Endless Fathoms
Potion: Battle Potion of Intellect or Potion of Rising Death
Food: Swamp Fish 'n Chips or Bountiful Captain's Feast. There are the cheaper counterparts as well; Ravenberry Tarts or Galley Banquet
Gems: Quick Owlseye or the cheaper option Quick Golden Beryl. Lastly you can use Kraken's Eye of Intellect.
