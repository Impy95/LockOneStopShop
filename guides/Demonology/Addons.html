  • Weak Auras 2
    The quintessential addon for tracking anything and everything there is in World of Warcraft. There are many places that you can find Weak Auras including:
    • wago.io
    • My website
    • Here on MMO-Champion
  • Ipse Shards
    DoomShards unfortunately has not worked properly for some time however there is a new Weak Aura by an amazing coder named Ipse who is replicating the functionality of DoomShards into a Weak Aura aptly called Ipse Shards which can be found by clicking on the link above.
