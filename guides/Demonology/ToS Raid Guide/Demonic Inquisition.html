Mythic - Demonic Inquisition

The main focus of this fight will be to maintain Doom on as many targets as possible. This includes both bosses and all adds that spawn throughout the encounter to try spawning as many Wild Imps as possible.
For the most bang for your buck it is recommended that you use Kazzak's Final Curse and Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning as your two legendaries as they synergize well with Impending Doom and the pure amount of demons you'll have out on average throughout the encounter to contribute towards Doom damage and additional summons of Doomguard.
Using your Demonic Circle effectively will be one of your best friends. Try keeping it off to the side so you can either portal out when Atrigan casts Calcified Quills on you or if you find yourself inside a Calcified Quills circle. This will reduce the amount of torment you actually gain throughout the encounter.
