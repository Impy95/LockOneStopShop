Mythic - Kil'jaeden

This fight is not very well suited for Demonology whatsoever. It is generally recommended to play Affliction or Destruction for this encounter but if you are deadset on playing Demonology you should be playing as if it is a single target fight.
With that being said you would be using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
Using your Demonic Circle efficiently to instantly portal back from the knockback will greatly reduce your overall movement.
