Mythic - Sisters of the Moon

Yet another fight great for Demonology as it's basically entirely single target. Outside of grouping up for a few mechanics you can simply stay in your assigned area for dropping the debuff and hit the boss without care.
You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
Using your Demonic Circle will be mostly specific to where you are dropping your debuff or if you want to use it as a way to easily get to the centre of the room for group mechanics.
