Mythic - Fallen Avatar

Another pure single target fight for Demonology but with some target swapping added. The best suggestion is to try and delay or have Dreadstalkers nearly available for every shield phase to give yourself that extra little bit of burst that will be required to break it.
On this fight you can help your raid a lot by utilizing your Demonic Circle well. Placing it in the back of the room allows you to teleport out of the way when targeted by a Shadowy Blade. This same teleport can also be used when the boss casts Unbound Chaos again to get out of the way.
You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
