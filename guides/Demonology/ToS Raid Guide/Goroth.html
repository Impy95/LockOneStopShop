Mythic - Goroth

There isn't much to say about this fight for Demonology as it's a pure single target fight.
You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
Using your Demonic Circle for times when Infernal Burning is cast will be a huge bonus so try to place your portal behind any of the pillars he spawns which will allow you to portal directly into a safe zone.
