Heroic - Coven of Shivarra

You'll see the best results using any combination of Kazzak's Final Curse, as first choice and one of the following Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
, Sephuz's Secret, The Master Harvester As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots. Sephuz's Secret can be considered if you can get a decent amount of uptime on Banish.

  • Save Doomguard/TKC/Service for Aman'thul adds, else use everything on cooldown pretty much.
  • If you get Norgannon phase early on consider running Sephuz and keep it banished for the rest of the fight to have maximum proc uptime (Careful it may backfire if your execution isn't on top).
  • Burning Rush makes you unaffected by Orb of Frost slow and by just DPSing the boss/adds you shouldn't take much damage if anything at all.
