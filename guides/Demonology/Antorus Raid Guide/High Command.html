Heroic - Antoran High Command

Demonology damage on this boss is focused mainly on boss (Heroic difficulty build) and passive cleaving targets around it (see Mythic difficulty build).

You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning, Kazzak's Final Curse or Recurrent Ritual.
As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Try to time 4 shard hog onto spawning adds, it does a surprising amount of frontloaded damage (don't interrupt the general gameplay flow though).
  • Whenever you can hit 4+ targets switch to DW over SB to generate shards.
  • If the adds on first and second pod spawn far from the boss consider sending felguard as a proxy for your DW to grab more shards (whenever feltroopers start charging away send it back to boss).
  • Not being able to proc sephuz here hurts demo dps. A LOT.
  • DP/UR make you pretty much immortal, cycle those wisely during incoming damage phases.
  • Don't trigger mines.
