Heroic - Essence of Eonar

Sephuz's Secret and Kazzak's Final Curse if you plan on aoe trash, Sephuz's Secret and Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty for priority targets.

  • You can proc Sephuz's Secret on Felhounds waves, so be sure to do so.
  • Demonology AoE here doesn't work better than other specs in terms of mechanics but you can still achieve some results with Implosion and Darkglare.
  • If waves die within seconds consider running a ST focused build to take down the big targets.
  • Try to take advantage of multiple targets clumped together to generate shards faster with Demonwrath.
  • Use Service on Destructors.

    Disclaimer: the unique benefit of Affliction in this fight is the ability to keep everything slowed forever with Absolute Corruption and Sacrolash (which outweights anything else basically), so if you don't have at least 2 Affliction Warlocks you should consider respeccing for this fight for progress.

    Mythic tips

  • Save Doomguard/Service/TKC for the Paraxis Inquisitor.
  • Red crystal debuff can be trivialized with Demonic Circle placement, just be sure to not propel other raid members in the air.
  • Green crystal debuff shouldn't be a problem at all considering Demonology's innate damage mitigation, be sure to cycle UR and DP if youre out of range of your healers.
