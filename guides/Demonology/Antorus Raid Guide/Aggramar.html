Heroic - Aggramar

You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Kazzak's Final Curse, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.
Sephuz's Secret can be considered if you can get a decent amount of uptime on Banish.

  • Pretty much single target fight without much added; add phases offers you the opportunity to keep one add banished for a period of time and benefit from Sephuz's Secret.
  • Place Demonic Circle either at the edges of the platform or where your group shares the boss cleave.

    • Mythic - Aggramar

    Mythic tips

  • Cooldown management/usage is mostly dependant on your raid DPS overall you may want to hold the first Doomguard reset when running Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning because right after the boss will phase out.
  • If you need more DPS during add phase consider delaying the 3rd Service and 4th doomguard to line up with the second add phase, if you phase too fast feel free to simply use both on CD on boss.
  • Try to save Doomguard/Service for when you pop BL/TW/Hero.
  • You may end up repositioning your Demonic circle after the first add phase since the boss is moved from its original spot.
