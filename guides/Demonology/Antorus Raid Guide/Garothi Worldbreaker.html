Heroic - Garothi Worldbreaker

You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Demonic Circle placement is key here, the less you move the more you can cast/the more damage you can do, and the Always Be Casting rule is extremely important for Demonology; if your guild stacks for Decimation place your Demonic Circle where the group stacks.
  • I position slightly behind the groups since as Demonology you have a pretty crazy damage mitigation and you require less heals in general (simple HoTs can keep you topped between each decimation).
  • Place the Demonic Gateway from the back of the room regroup to where your melee stand to help get them out of the Eradication ability.

    • Mythic - Garothi Worldbreaker

  • Demonic Circle can be used to soak twice the Annihilation since it will reset everytime, just be sure to place it on top of the one youre soaking and teleport right after you get knocked back the first time.
