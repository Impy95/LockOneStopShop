Heroic - Argus the Unmaker

You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Kazzak's Final Curse, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning or Wakener's Loyalty.
As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Use Demonic Circle/Gateway to severely reduce the downtime in case you're targeted by Soul Bomb/Soul Burst.
  • During add phase if you really want to min-max the damage you should send Felguard/Service on physical add, cast Shadowbolt, Dreadstalkers, Doomguard and TKC on Shadow add and finally HoG on Fire add (results may vary depending on your raid comp, generally physical add dies almost instantly).

    • Mythic - Argus the Unmaker

  • Unless running very high Haste and low Mastery consider stacking Gift of the Sea during P1; remember to pick the opposite buff when it spawns first before taking the one you will want to stack the moment Argus casts its first cone.
  • Especially if running Wakener consider holding TKC for modules, unless you already have enough raid DPS to deal with those.
