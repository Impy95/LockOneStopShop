Heroic - Kin'garoth

You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning, Wakener's Loyalty or Kazzak's Final Curse.
As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Try to get into add phase with full shards everytime.
  • Use only TKC/Felstorm at the beginning of the encounter, and save Service/DG/TKC for every add phase.
  • Place your Demonic Circle in melee in between the 2 add platform youre going to do, and use it to trivialize Ruiner, just be sure there arent many crystals on top of it, in which case just reposition it.
  • You can hit adds from range if you stand in melee boss range as long as youre right in front of those adds.
  • Manually reposition your Felguard to be as close as the next add as possible when hitting the boss, to minimize DPS loss (if you do it correctly Felguard will charge almost immediately).
