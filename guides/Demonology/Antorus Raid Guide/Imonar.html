Heroic - Imonar the Soulhunter

You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning, Wakener's Loyalty or Kazzak's Final Curse.
As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Before the fight start you can place both Gateway and Circle to make bridge transition easier for your group; try to put the Gateway on 1 side of the bridge close to the entrance to minimize the amount of possible mines spawning in its path.

    • Mythic - Imonar the Soulhunter

  • If you play correctly you should have double TKC and Service/DG (if you play with Wilfred) for every platform.
  • Try to minimize movement as much as you can during each platform phase.
  • If you run with DP (which I highly suggest) on top of being able to mitigate the damage from Empowered Pulse Grenade each bridge transition you can also use it to soak the mines in the 3rd platform without putting stress to your healers.
