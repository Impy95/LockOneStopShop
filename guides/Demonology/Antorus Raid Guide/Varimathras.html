Heroic - Varimathras

You'll see the best results using any combination of the more 'single target' oriented legendaries including; Recurrent Ritual, Wilfred's Sigil of Superior Summoning, Wakener's Loyalty or Kazzak's Final Curse.
As usual the legendary choice depends on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Try to plan and minimize movements as much as you can.

    • Mythic - Varimathras

  • Your ranged add damage will be quite pitiful and your consistently reliable spell will be the instant Shadowbolt which tickles anyway. It is better to position your Felguard manually between the 2 melee flares so it can cleave hopefully even the ranged add (assuming tanks can drag it in range).
  • Some attempts you will have lot of add damage but that's mainly a combination of TKD proc at the right time and lucky SC resets that make you splash 4 shards HoG on spawning adds; if your raid need add damage consider swapping to Destruction.
