Legendaries

Prydaz is highly recommended on this fight. The dps check isn't THAT hard to meet, the primary check is just not dying long enough to kill the boss. For your second legendary, you'll probably get the most dps out of Helm or Belt. I recommend simming your character over at Raidbots.

Tips/Tricks

  • You can put your Demonic Circle on one of the platforms and use it during transitions to skip large parts of the bridge.
  • Many guilds opt to use a Demonic Gateway to aid in crossing the bridge during transitions. Try to always places yours in the same location if asked to.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • Nothing changes for warlocks on mythic. More damage incoming, more mechanics to dodge. Don't stand in fire and collect loot.
