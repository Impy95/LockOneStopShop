Talents

Generic Single Target talents. On mythic, it's highly worth considering SC over SL. The adds on mythic give a ton of free shards which pump the value of SC up.

Legendaries

This is just going to come down to the gear you have available. Varimathras is as close to patchwerk as you get this tier, so I recommend simming your character over at Raidbots to figure out which legendaries will be the best for you.

It should be noted that on mythic the excess shards from Drain Souling adds reduce the value of Belt.

Tips/Tricks

  • There's no real tips or tricks to give for this fight on heroic. You pretty much just sit there and hit buttons until the boss dies.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • Pay close attention to the add timers. Try to dump your shards in such a way that you'll never cap from draining the adds and you won't have to interrupt an MG cycle to drain them. Because they live for such a short period, your damage is going to suck on them and you're best off just using Drain Soul on them to get more ST.
