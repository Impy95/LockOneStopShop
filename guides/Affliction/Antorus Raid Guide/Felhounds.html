Talents

I recommend AC for progression. Contagion can net you more damage, but only if the bosses are consistently within your range and you can juggle 8 dots without screwing up mechanics.

Legendaries

There are a wide variety of legendaries that give similar dps on this fight: Helm, Belt, Sacrolash, Prydaz, Bracers. I recommend simming your character over at Raidbots on 2 bosses.

Keep in mind, Belt and Bracers lose some value if your guild doesn't position bosses closely enough to always multidot.

Tips/Tricks

  • Not much to say for heroic. Don't stand in bad, keep your dots up.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • Your dps is going to depend heavily on how far apart your guild keeps the bosses, and how unlucky you are with being targeted by Flametouched on pull.
  • Place your Demonic Circle in the center of the room for quick recentering after Siphon Corruption.
  • When you have Flametouched, make sure you refresh your Agony/SL on Shadow Dog before Siphon Corruption; the boss may go out of range while you soak.
