Felhounds of Sargeras

Talents

I recommend AC for progression. Contagion can net you more damage, but only if the bosses are consistently within your range and you can juggle 8 dots without screwing up mechanics.

Legendaries

There are a wide variety of legendaries that give similar dps on this fight: Helm, Belt, Sacrolash, Prydaz, Bracers. I recommend simming your character over at Raidbots on 2 bosses.

Keep in mind, Belt and Bracers lose some value if your guild doesn't position bosses closely enough to always multidot.

Tips/Tricks

  • Not much to say for heroic. Don't stand in bad, keep your dots up.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • Your dps is going to depend heavily on how far apart your guild keeps the bosses, and how unlucky you are with being targeted by Flametouched. Ideally, you get shadow everytime and the bosses are always in range.
  • If you end up getting Flametouched and your guild doesn't keep the dogs close enough to always multidot while stacking for Siphon Corruption, place your Demonic Circle in the center of the room for quick recentering after soaks and refresh your Agony/SL on the Shadow Dog before or after Siphon Corruption.
