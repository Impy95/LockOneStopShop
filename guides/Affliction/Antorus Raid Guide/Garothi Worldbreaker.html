Talents

Generic Single Target talents. Dark Pact makes soaking easier if you feel safe without Burning Rush.

Legendaries

Helm and Belt are the standard ST legendaries. However, Sephuz and Prydaz both have potential to be your best dps legendary ST. I recommend simming your character over at Raidbots.

Tips/Tricks

  • If your guild stacks for Decimation, place your Demonic Circle where the group stacks. It will be off cooldown for every Decimation.
  • If your guild doesn't stack for Decimation, place your Demonic Circle somewhere in the back of the room away from the raid, and where your tank runs for Fel Bombardment.
  • Place a Demonic Gateway from the back of the room to where your melee stand to help them get out for Eradication.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • Nothing really changes for Mythic. Make sure you try to aim yourself to get knocked back near another Annihilation for the second soak after killing the Annihilator.
