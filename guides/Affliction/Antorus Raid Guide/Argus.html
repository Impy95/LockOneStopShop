Argus the Unmaker

Talents

Generic Single Target talents. If your guild is struggling with killing the Celestial adds, consider swapping to Writhe and potentially even AC.

Legendaries

Several legendaries are very close ST, I recommend simming your character over at Raidbots to find which work best with your gear. It should be noted that Argus has a hard execute burn phase, so you should consider using Netherlord.

Tips/Tricks

  • Place your Demonic Circle wherever your raid stacks to get back into position after any bombs you might get.
  • Consider putting a Demonic Gateway from the raid's stack spot to wherever the big bombs are sent.
  • The shadow add will always spawn in the same general area, learn where it is and never forget. None of the other adds buff your damage in any way.
