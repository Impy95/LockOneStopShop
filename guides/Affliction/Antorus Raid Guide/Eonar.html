Talents

This is the once-a-tier joke fight. It really doesn't matter what talents or legendaries or anything you take or do really. I recommend the following because it gives you permanent slow for adds while maintaining high ST for Obfuscators and Destructors.

Legendaries

Sacrolash is obviously required for permanent 60% slow on adds. Most people run Sephuz for their second legendary because you can proc it with Howl of Terror and Infernal Stun on Felhounds.

Tips/Tricks

  • Don't scumbag. No one cares about Eonar rankings and the only thing that even remotely matters is damage to Obfuscators and Destructors.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • I've only ever done red crystal for Final Doom, so I can't really speak for how the other debuffs might change how you play. With red you basically just stand away from people and lose dps from getting knocked up.
