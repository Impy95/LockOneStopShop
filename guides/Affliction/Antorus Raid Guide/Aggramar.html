Legendaries

Sephuz is a staple for heroic. You can proc it via banishing adds.
For your second legendary, you'll probably get the most dps out of Helm or Belt. I recommend simming your character over at Raidbots.

Tips/Tricks

  • Find a place no one else is standing, put your Demonic Circle down, and always go back to that location.
  • The add your designated to banish won't always spawn in the same place, so pay close attention as there spawning to which is closest to the area you're assigned.
  • Try to time your DoT refreshing around running in to soak Flame Rend.

Mythic

  • There are several strats you can use for mythic aggramar. The primary two are using DKs and mass CCs to control the add explosions, and permanently slowing the adds via affliction warlocks. Which strat you use will heavily alter how you prepare and play.
  • Mass CC Strategy: Generic ST legendaries and talents. There's some debate about SL vs SC and if you use Netherlord or not. I'm personally in the camp of SL and Netherlord. The 40-0 Phase is the hardest part of the fight.
  • Permanent Slows Strategy: Writhe/AC/SH and either DE or SC. You definitely want either DE or Netherlord. The basic gist is that because you can dot the adds, you get way more value out of SC/DE and Writhe get more value than SL or MG.
