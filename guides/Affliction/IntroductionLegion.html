Now that all that stuff is out of the way, actual guide:

Affliction's Strengths:

  • Single Target on fights with adds via Wrath of Consumption
  • The best multi-dotting in the game
  • Sustained AoE via Sow the Seeds for Mythic+
  • Requires less healing than any other dps spec

Affliction's Weaknesses:

  • Has decent burst via Unstable Affliction spamming, but requires a bit of ramp time to get full damage going.
  • Heavily balanced around adds. Affliction can be pretty mediocre on fights without adds or multiple bosses.
  • Relatively low skillcap and high RNG means the spec is easy to pick up and master, but once you do it's a bit of gambling.
  • Affliction has generally good mobility, except on Single Target fights. Malefic Grasp is a massive portion of our ST and gets punished very hard by movement.
