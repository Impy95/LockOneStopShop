Quick Reference

This should only be used as a quick reference in a general sense for your entry into Mythic+ Dungeons.

Hard Tyrannical Bosses

You have a few choices to make depending on your own preference or depending on the bosses throughout the dungeon you're in:
You can either play with Soul Harvest or Phantom Singularity and Demonic Embrace or Soul Conduit. You also have a choice between Burning Rush and Dark Pact but you should normally just play Burning Rush considering you are able to full heal with Drain Soul very easily.

AoE Rotations

Contagion + Soul Harvest/Phantom Singularity

  • Pre-cast Seed of Corruption if possible.

  • Agony on all targets that will live longer than 5 seconds, refresh as needed.

  • Use Seed of Corruption to maintain Corruption on anything over 2 targets - manually apply Corrupiton on anything with 2 or less targets.

  • Phantom Singularity or Soul Harvest on cooldown unless you are saving for a specific pull or boss.

  • Spread Unstable Affliction on every target (or dump them into one target if there is priority damage required).

  • Drain Soul targets that are about to die unless you are shard capped.

Empowered Life Tap + Sow the Seeds

  • Pre-cast Seed of Corruption if possible.

  • Cast Life Tap to maintain Empowered Life Tap at all times (unless mounted or invisible).

  • Agony up to 4-5 targets that will live longer than 5s, refresh as needed.

  • Use Seed of Corruption to maintain Corruption on anything over 2 targets - manually apply Corrupiton on anything with 2 or less targets.

  • Drain Soul targets that are about to die unless you are shard capped.

Pets!

You will essentially choose your pet based on what utility is needed for your group:
If you need a stun: Infernal
If you need an interrupt: Doomguard

There may be situations where you will choose your pet based on how you can proc Sephuz. For example there are some mobs that are immune to stuns but can be interrupted or situations where there is nothing you can interrupt but you can stun the mobs.

If, for some reason, the utility your pet can bring is not needed always keep in mind that Infernal does more damage when you have 1.6 or more targets but be careful and be sure to watch over your pet as you could pull additional mobs due to the radious of the AoE from Infernal or from a mob patrolling while your Doomguard sits in the back and casts.
