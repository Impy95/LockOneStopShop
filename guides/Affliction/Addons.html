While there are many addons out there, there are certainly a few that stick out as great ones, here are some that I highly recommend:
  • Weak Auras 2
    The quintessential addon for tracking anything and everything there is in World of Warcraft. There are many places that you can find Weak Auras including:
    • wago.io has a variety of options that you can explore.
    • This is Not's aura suite, it has all specs included in one aura group.
    • Kirin has also made a set of his own.
    • Ipse has always made a great set of auras for Warlock.
  • Azeroth Auto Pilot
    This leveling addon has been vetted by numerous "speed levelers" and even I have used it multiple times to assist in the leveling process. A great way to figure out where to go and when without thinking about it at all!
  • Auto Loot Plus
    Another leveling/general purpose addon that speeds up the looting process. How fast does it speed it up? It makes looting nearly instant, you'll basically never see the loot window.
  • Ipse Shards
    DoomShards unfortunately has not worked properly since sometime in Legion, however there is a new Weak Aura by an amazing coder named Ipse who is replicating the functionality of DoomShards into a Weak Aura aptly called Ipse Shards which can be found by clicking on the link above.
