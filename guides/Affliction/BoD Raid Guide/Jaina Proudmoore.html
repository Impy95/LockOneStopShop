Heroic - Jaina Proudmoore

This is a multi-phased primarily single target encounter with some priority add burns. Phase 1 is pretty much entirely single target as adds are either Mind Controlled off the boat or obliterated before you can deal any significant damage to them. Phase 2 has more targets, namely ice blocks and ordinances that must be burned. Frozen ordinances can be used to generate shards and ID stacks with agony. Phase 3 is 3 separate burst adds (Wall, Natahanos, Tidewater Elemental). Phase 4 is a single target burn with ranged priority adds (Prismatic Images), and ice blocks. Since most adds will not live long enough for agony to ramp, Siphon Life is the go-to talent. Inevitable Demise is useful for quickly bursting ordinances, ice wall, tide elemental, and prismatic images, otherwse normal single-target traits take priority. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Maintain dots on all targets and focus the sirens and storm elementals as needed until you or the raid leader decide to burn the boss.
  • Shadowfury can be used to stun the raptor adds.
  • You should use imp for this encounter to help with swapping between adds and boss. If you're in desperate need of a slow for the storm elementals the succubus has a 30% slow.
  • Gateway can be quite useful in p1 to clear fire on far ballistas, and in p2 and p3 to reposition around icy patches and get to ordinances and images.
