Tier

Our tier 20 is the Diabolic Raiment.

  • 2 piece: Gives your Corruption a 0.65 RPPM chance to proc a soulshard. This doesn't scale with number of targets, because it's RPPM.
  • 4 piece: Gives your shard spenders a 25% chance to proc a 15% haste buff for 8 seconds.

Our tier 21 is the Grim Inquisitor's Regalia.

  • 2 piece: Gives your Agony an 8% chance whenever it deals damage to increase the duration of any Unstable Afflictions on the target by 2 seconds.
  • 4 piece: Everytime you cast Unstable Affliction of Seed of Corruption, applies a debuff to all targets afflicted with an Agony within 60 yards which increases the damage of Agony, Corruption and Siphon Life by 10% for 8 seconds.

Our T20 4pc is quite good, but the 2pc is very bad. Because of this, it may be worthwhile to hold on to your T20 4pc for heroic week. Once you get T21 that's a about 15 ilevel higher than your T20, you should drop your T20 entirely, as the 2pc is weak.

Our T21 2pc is incredibly powerful, and actually worth more than our 4pc. 4pc scales very well on cleave fights to compensate.

Legendaries

What to Upgrade first

I recommend that your first 3 upgrades be helm, belt and prydaz. If you get your t21 4pc this week, go ahead and upgrade your helm and belt. If you don't get t21 4pc, upgrade belt and prydaz.

The best Legendaries

  • Affliction legendaries are pretty well balanced, so which you use will depend heavily on your raid composition and the individual fight. In general, Helm and Belt are of similar value and your best choices for raw dps.
  • Soul of the Netherlord is slightly lower overall dps than the legendaries above, but gives you INCREDIBLY powerful execute. Unlike previous tiers, you're pretty much never going to use this for fights you take Siphon Life on, because the 2 talents have very little synergy. It's still a very strong choice for fights where you'd typically run SC over SL.
  • Sacrolashs Dark Strike is BiS on a few fights where you have many targets out for long periods of time in conjunction with Absolute Corruption. Namely Coven of Shivarra. The slow is also incredibly useful on multiple fights.
  • Prydaz, Xavaric's Magnum Opus is only a few percent (1-2% depending on gear) behind the better legendaries, and provides a large increase to survivability. It's always a solid choice for progression, particularly on fights that are very healing intensive.
  • Streten's Sleepless Shackles has some niche uses for cleave fights where you want to run contagion. It's not recommended for beginners because it's pretty hard to juggle agony and corruption on multiple targets and also cycle UAs. The only notable case for this I can think of is Felhounds.
  • Sephuz's Secret and The Master Harvester are both very powerful in conjunction with Sow the Seeds for things like Mythic+.

Relic Choices
