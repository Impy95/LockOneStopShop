Now that all that stuff is out of the way, actual guide:

Affliction's Strengths:

  • Strong Single Target on fights with adds via Wrath of Consumption
  • Extremely strong multi-dotting
  • Incredible mobility with a large amount of our damage coming from instant cast dots.

Affliction's Weaknesses:

  • Has decent burst via UA spamming, but requires a bit of ramp time to get full damage going.
  • Heavily balanced around adds. Affliction can be pretty mediocre on fights without adds or multiple bosses.
  • Relatively low skillcap and high RNG means the spec is easy to pick up and master, but once you do it's a bit of gambling.

7.2.5 Changes

Legendaries

  • The Master Harvester has been added
  • Soul of the Netherlord has been added.

Artifact

  • Wrath of Consumption has been nerfed from 2% per stack to 1% per stack. This was done in an attempt to make affliction better on fights without adds, and less op on fights with adds.

Talents

  • Soul Effigy has been removed.
  • Death's Embrace has replaced Soul Effigy as the level 100 talent. It is a ramping execute, providing 0% bonus damage when the target is at 35%, and 50% bonus damage when the target hits 0%.
  • Siphon Life and Phantom Singularity swapped places. Siphon Life also received multiple changes:
    • Damage increased by 30%.
    • Healing received down from 100% to 60% of damage.
  • Phantom Singularity received multiple changes:
    • Cooldown dropped from 60 to 40 seconds.
    • Damage increased by 5%.
    • Healing done reduced from 30% to 20% damage dealt.
  • Contagion reduced from 18% to 15%.
  • Malefic Grasp reduced from 70% to 25%.
  • Haunt received multiple changes:
    • Cooldown reduced from 30 to 25.
    • Damage reduced from 805% spelldamage to 500%.
    • Damage Amplifier reduced from 30% to 15%.
    • Duration reduced from 15 seconds to 10 seconds.
  • Writhe in Agony reduced from 20 max stacks to 15 max stacks.
  • Soul Harvest received multiple changes:
    • Base duration reduced from 15 seconds to 12 seconds.
    • Duration added per Agony increased from 2 to 4 seconds.

Spells

  • All affliction damage increased by 14%.
  • The following spells have received specific nerfs in addition to the above 14% buff.
    • Soul Flame nerfed by 20%, net -8.8% from 7.2.
    • Seed of Corruption nerfed by 20%, net -8.8% from 7.2.
    • Agony nerfed by 5%, net +8.3% from 7.2.
    • Corruption nerfed by 5%, net +8.3% from 7.2.
