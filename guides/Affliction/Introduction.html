Now that all that stuff is out of the way, the actual guide:

Affliction's Strengths:

  • Single Target damage
  • Spread Cleave damage
  • Extremely mobile
  • Multiple strong Cooldowns

Affliction's Weaknesses:

  • Bad Area of Effect damage. Seed is very weak for a baseline AoE spell.
  • Short-lived adds. You won't be doing much damage to things that live for less than 10 seconds or so. (E.g. Explosive Affix)
