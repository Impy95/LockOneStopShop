Intro to Ulthalesh, the Deadwind Harvester

First thing to know is how your artifact power Reap Souls works.

  • First, there are Tormented Soul that will periodically appear as you deal damage to targets. These spawn at 5 RPPM. These will appear as little purple balls floating around your charactor.
  • Tormented Soul will also spawn each time a mob that you've dealt damage to dies. These will appear as the ghost of the mob you have recently killed. A KILLING BLOW IS NOT REQUIRED FOR THIS EFFECT.
  • The Tormented Soul buff will stack up to 12 stacks, and each individual Tormented Soul will last at most 2 minutes before despawning.
  • Hitting Reap Souls will consume all Tormented Souls, applying the Reap Souls buff for 5 seconds per soul consumed. This increasing all damage you deal by 10%, and doubles the value of your artifact traits.

There are a few traits that don't work in very obvious ways.

  • Wrath of Consumption: A buff that procs when something you've damaged dies. Each stack increases your damage by 1%.
  • Soul Flame: Causes the corpses of enemies you've damaged to explode on death, dealing a large amount of damage in an area around their corpse.
  • Fatal Echoes: Gives any Unstable Affliction applied to an enemy a chance to reapply itself when it expires. Think of this as a free bonus Unstable Affliction. You can't really play around it, it's just free damage.
  • Rend Soul: Gives damage from Drain Soul a chance to deal a small amount of damage and generate a Tormented Soul
Leveling your artifact

This is the artifact path while leveling and starting the gearing process. It's important that you fill in Rend Soul before upgrading any other traits for 4/4.

Continuing the empowerment

Once you have leveled through to Rend Soul, you can begin to fill the 3/4 traits to 4/4.
