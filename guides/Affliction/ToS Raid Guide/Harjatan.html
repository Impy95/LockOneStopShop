Harjatan

Talents

For heroic you generally just want to maximize your ST damage. On mythic swap to Writhe in Agony. If your raid needs damage for Sickly Tadpoles, consider swapping to Sow the Seeds and Absolute Corruption.

Legendaries

Helm, Chest, and Netherlord are ideal on this fight. If your raid needs a slow for adds, use Sacrolash.

Tips/Tricks

  • Pay attention to your Commanding Roar timer and be ready to target and agony the murlocs ASAP.
  • If you're using Soul Harvest, delay it's use for adds to increase it's duration. Try to use your last one when the boss is at low health to maximize Death's Embrace value.
