Fallen Avatar

Talents

Legendaries

Helm, Chest, Netherlord, and Belt are all about equal on this fight.
Prydaz is a strong consideration here; a lot of Avatar's mechanics deal large amounts of burst damage.

Tips/Tricks

  • Depending on your raid group, you may need to slow down or increase damage on the Maiden at certain points in the fight. You can slow your damage on her by other not Agonying at all, or letting Agony fall off before refreshing it to reset its stacks.
  • Place your Demonic Circle in the back of the room away from other players for easier placement of Shadowy Blades and Unbound Chaos.
