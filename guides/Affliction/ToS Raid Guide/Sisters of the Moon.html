Sisters of the Moon

Talents

Generic Single Target talents.

Legendaries

Netherlord is recommended on this fight as the last phase has the most relevant dps check. The tier 19 2 piece bonus shines on this fight. Because of that, most people opt to run Belt. If you don't have good tier 19 2 piece items, opt for Helm or Chest.
I personally prefer Prydaz for this fight. It's certainly a bit less DPS than the other legendaries, but there's a lot of stuff that can one shot you on Sisters.

Tips/Tricks

  • Remember to use your GCDs while moving to stack for Incorporeal Shot refreshing your dots or life tapping.
  • Place your Demonic Circle near where you usually stand to reduce time moving out after Incorporeal Shot.
  • The 9 small glaives from Glaive Storm after the second split can be completely avoided by standing on the walls.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • If you're assigned to the first clear group, place your Demonic Circle on the right most wall so that you can clear stacks instantly.
  • Make a Demonic Gateway from left wall to right wall or vice versa. This helps people clear Moon Burns.
  • Save your personals incase you get a Twilight Glaive at high debuff stacks. This will probably happen to you at least once in the fight.
