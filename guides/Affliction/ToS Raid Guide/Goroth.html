Goroth

Talents

Generic Single Target talents. Dark Pact makes soaking easier if you feel safe without Burning Rush.

Legendaries

The tier 19 2 piece bonus shines on this fight. Because of that, most people opt to run Belt and Netherlord. If you don't have good tier 19 2 piece items, Helm and Chest are also good choices.

Tips/Tricks

  • Place your gateway crossing the middle of the room between 2 quadrants. This reduces the odds someone gets caught out from a pillar for Infernal Burning

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • Place your Demonic Circle in a quadrant you aren't assigned to soak. If no infernals spawn in your quadrant, this makes it easier to soak in others.
