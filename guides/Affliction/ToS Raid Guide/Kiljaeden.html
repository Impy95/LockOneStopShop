Kil'jaeden

Talents

For heroic you generally just want to maximize your ST damage. On mythic, Writhe in Agony pulls ahead because of adds and excessive movement.

Legendaries

Netherlord is recommended to help with the execute phase. Helm, Belt, and Chest are all good second choices.

Tips/Tricks

  • Place your Demonic Circle near where you normally stand. In case you're ever at risk of getting knocked off, use your Demonic Circle after the knockback starts.
  • Place your Demonic Gateway between 2 corners of the room. Make sure you don't put it directly through the center; if you gateway through a center orb it one shots you. The ideal place is just slightly to the side of the center.
  • Start precasting Seed of Corruption on the boss when the adds are ~2 seconds from spawning. This way, you apply corruption instantly to all 3.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • Warlocks can pretty easily take 2 debuffs during primary phases because of Drain Soul. However, ANYONE taking 2+ stacks during intermissions is sketchy.
  • Make sure you cancel your burning rush if you have Lingering Eruption. You need to run at base movement speed to spawn orbs in the correct way.
