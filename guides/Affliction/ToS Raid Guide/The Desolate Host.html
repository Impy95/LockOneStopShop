The Desolate Host

Talents

Affliction Warlocks can maintain Corruption on both bosses the entire fight via Absolute Corruption.

Legendaries

Sacrolash is the obvious best legendary on this fight. For your second legendary, Helm, Chest, and Netherlord are of similar value.

Tips/Tricks

  • Start on the side you aren't assigned to (on mythic make sure you use life tap / burning rush to reduce your health down to 20% or so to prevent smart heals from pushing you over 50%). Apply Corruption via Seed of Corruption to the targets and then immediately use the font to go to your assigned side.
  • Place your Demonic Circle wherever your guild has designated to clear Soulbind.
  • If you're in the Spirit Realm and a Blood DK is tanking for you, use Seed of Corruption as they Mass Grip the adds to apply Corruption to them all.
  • Maintain Agony on as many targets as possible, but keep in mind the Soul Residues (little voidlings) often die too fast for Agony to be worth it on them.
  • If you're in the Spirit Realm, make sure you save Soul Harvest on the pull until the adds have spawned.

Mythic Tips/Tricks

  • If you're in the Corporeal Realm, talent Dark Pact so you don't get killed by Spear of Anguish.
  • Warlocks can relatively easily get back to Corporeal Realm if hit by Spear of Anguish by spamming Life Tap. I'd also recommend getting a macro that cancels common healer HoTs.
