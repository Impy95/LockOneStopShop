What is Lock One Stop Shop?

Lock One Stop Shop is your one place for everything Warlock. Whether you are just starting out as a Warlock or trying to understand all the nitty gritty details we have information for everyone. Curated specifically by top players so you can feel comfortable knowing the information is accurate.

We are dedicated to providing you with all the latest information as soon as it's available regarding everything you might need to know for PvE. You won't find any "memecrafting" or "feelycrafting" as everything on the site is backed up with sims, math or hivemind collaboration.
