Foreword

Warlock is currently a diverse and powerful class, with 3 specs that fill unique roles while being enjoyable to play. The issues with the class can be mostly boiled down to 2 main categories: Talent Balance, and Bugs.

A reccuring theme across every warlock spec is that our talent balance is extremely flawed in many places. This leads to holes in each spec’s capabilities that certain talents should fill; burst talents for Demonology, single target talents for Destruction, and add management talents for Affliction.

This series of posts is ever-evolving based on feedback from the community. We've added a changelog so you can stay up-to-date on any and all changes made to it..

However, Affliction's spec aura still doesn't apply to pets.


As you can see, my imp deals 732 damage. I have 1464 spell power, which means my imp has 1464 * 1.25 = 1830. The spell power coefficient of Firebolt is 40%. 1830 * 0.4 = 732. If the spec aura was properly applying to pets, you’d get a result of 732 * 1.25 = 915.

This results in large imbalances in the value of Grimoire of Sacrifice between Destruction and Affliction. Destruction’s aura applies to both, but Affliction’s only applies to Grimoire of Sacrifice. It also means a significantly larger percentage of Affliction's damage comes from its spells, making it scale much better than intended on cleave/aoe. We think it's extremely important this gets fixed, as a majority of the fights in the upcoming raid are spreadcleave; moving damage from Affliction's spells into its pets will cut back the massive lead it has on spreadcleave compared to most other specs in the game.

Proposed Pet Rebalancing

  • Make Affliction’s spec aura properly apply to pets (Effect 191032 Base Value 0->25)
  • Nerf Sumon Darkglare’s Eye Beam by 25% (0.32->0.256) to keep its value the same as live
  • Nerf Affliction’s spec aura to 21% (25->21)
  • Increase Destruction's spec aura to -15% (-16->-15)
  • Given the nerfs to specific Affliction talents suggested later, leave Affliction's spec aura at 25%.
  • Given the buffs to specific Destruction talents suggested later, reduce Destruction's spec aura to -17% (-16->-17)

All Proposed Changes

Pets

  • Make Affliction’s spec aura properly apply to pets (Effect 191032 Base Value 0->25)
  • Nerf Sumon Darkglare’s Eye Beam by 25% (0.32->0.256) to keep its value the same as live
  • Nerf Affliction’s spec aura to 22% (25->22)
  • Increase Destruction's spec aura to -15% (-16->-15)
  • Given the changes to specific Affliction talents suggested later, leave Affliction's spec aura at 25%
  • Given the changes to specific Destruction talents suggested later, reduce Destruction's spec aura to -18% (-16->-18)

Affliction

  • Deathbolt percentage of remaining damage reduced to 25% (30->25)
  • Drain Soul damage increased by 40% (0.15->0.21)
  • Nightfall's proc chance now scales off of Haste
  • Nightfall's proc chance increased to 4 RPPM (3->4)
  • Nightfall's damage bonus increased to 80% (20->80)
  • Shadow Embrace's duration increased to 15 seconds (10->15)
  • Grimoire of Sacrifice damage increased by 37% (0.35->0.48)
  • Dark Soul: Misery's effect reduced to 25% haste (30->25)
  • Soul Conduit's proc rate increased to 20% (15->20)
  • Creeping Death's effect increased to 20% (15->20)
  • Consider a further nerf to Affliction's aura, as the spec is already justifying of a nerf on Live in our opinion, and these changes make it more mechanically diverse.

Demonology

  • Demonic Consumpion's energy consumed per 5% damage reduced to 8 (10->8)
  • Increased the damage of Demons randomly summoned through Inner Demons and Nether Portal by 20%
  • From the Shadows effect increased to 50% (20->50)

Destruction

  • Soul Fire damage increased by 60% (1.0->1.60)
  • Internal Combustion now consumes 6 seconds of Immolate, OR new effect "instantly dealing 120% of damage consumed."
  • Shadowburn damage increased by 55% (0.6->0.93)
  • Fire and Brimstone damage percentage increased to 65% (40->65)
  • Inferno's proc chance increased to 30% (20->30)
  • Roaring Blaze's damage increased by 25% (0.16->0.2)
  • Grimoire of Sacrifice damage increased by 37% (0.35->0.48)
  • Channel Demonfire's damage to its primary target increased by 25% (0.16->0.2)
  • Soul Conduit's proc rate increased to 20% (15->20)
  • Reduce Destruction's spec aura to -18% (-15->-18)
