While there are many addons out there, there are certainly a few that stick out as great ones, here are some that I highly recommend:
  • Weak Auras 2
    The quintessential addon for tracking anything and everything there is in World of Warcraft. There are many places that you can find Weak Auras including:
    • wago.io, specifically my auras can be found here: Loozy's Destruction Warlock Weakauras.
    • Not's BFA Warlock Auras
    • Ipse's WA's
    • Kirin's BFA Warlock Auras
  • ElvUI

    I would also recommend using a user interface or UI addon. The one I suggest using is called ElvUI. Elvui allows you to customize pretty much everything in your interface, you can download my ElvUI profile here Loozy's Elvui Profile.

  • Details or Skada

    Skada or Details are the perfect addons to let you check the damage you and your raid member do. These addons are also useful to track interrupts, dispels or deaths.

  • BigWigs or Deadly Boss Mods

    BossMods are addons that show you timers for upcoming boss abilities and make noises if you stand in fire. You can choose between Deadly Boss Mods and BigWigs. I would recommend BigWigs.
