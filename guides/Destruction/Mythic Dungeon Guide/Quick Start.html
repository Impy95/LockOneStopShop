Hard Tyrannical Bosses

You have a few choices to make depending on your own preference or depending on the bosses throughout the dungeon you're in:
You can either play with Eradication or Empowered Life Tap and Soul Harvest and Fire and Brimstone. You can also choose between Burning Rush and Dark Pact but you will normally play Burning Rush.

Fortified, Teeming or Easy Tyrannical Bosses

Legendaries

Trash:
Sephuz's Secret as the primary legendary.
The Master Harvester, Lessons of Space-Time or Alythess Pyrogenics as the secondary legendary.

Bosses:
Standard single target legendaries that you would use during a raid, etc.
Sindorei Spite becomes more valuable in mythic+ due to shorter boss fights than in raids.

AoE Rotations

3-4 Targets

  • Maintain ELT

  • Maintain Immolate on all targets

  • Havoc on cooldown, use with Conflagrate and Chaos Bolt. Ideal havocs consist of 2xConflagrate and 3xChaos Bolt.

  • Channel Demonfire and Conflagrate on cooldown

  • Use Rain of Fire if Havoc is on cooldown to spend shards.

5+ Targets

  • Maintain ELT

  • Immolate 1 target for Channel Demonfire

  • Channel Demonfire and Conflagrate on cooldown

  • Spam Rain of Fire to spend shards.

Pets!

Destruction primarily uses Imp with Grimoire of Service.
There may be situations where an interrupt is needed from you even when you are using Service (Medivh in Upper Karazan). If this is the case use felhunter to make sure the interrupt is handled

If you are using Grimoire of Supremacy you will use either Infernal or Doomguard based on what you need:
If you need a stun: Infernal
If you need an interrupt: Doomguard
Keep in mind that Infernal does more damage when you have 1.6 or more targets but be careful and be sure to watch over your pet as you could pull additional mobs due to the radious of the AoE from Infernal or from a mob patrolling while your Doomguard sits in the back and casts.
