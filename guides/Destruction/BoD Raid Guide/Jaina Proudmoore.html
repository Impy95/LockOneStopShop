Heroic - Jaina Proudmoore

Eradication and Internal Combustion is better than Flashover and Reverse Entropy in theory if you are up for the added maintenance of Immolate and Eradication throughout the fight, but most opt to just use FO and RE.

This is a multi-phased primarily single target encounter with some priority add burns. Phase 1 is pretty much entirely single target as adds are either Mind Controlled off the boat or obliterated before you can deal any significant damage to them. Phase 2 has more targets, namely ice blocks and ordinances that must be burned. Phase 3 is 3 separate burst adds (Ice Wall, Nathanos, Tidewater Elemental). Phase 4 is a single target burn with ranged priority adds (Prismatic Images), and ice blocks. Since there are so many targets that will get ticked at least once above 80% health, Flashpoint will be very strong for this fight. Outside of Flashpoint, your general single target traits will be best. As usual your azerite and trinket choices depend on the gear you have available, be sure to sim your character over at Raidbots.

  • Unexploded Ordnance barrels can be used to generate shards and Flashpoint uptime with Immolate, make sure to watch damage on them and stop dots if needed as early breaks can cause wipes.
  • Imp can dispel the Grasp of Frost and is also the best pet for swapping targets.
  • Gateway can be quite useful in p1 to clear fire on far ballistas and in p2 and p3 to reposition around icy patches and get to ordnances and images.
