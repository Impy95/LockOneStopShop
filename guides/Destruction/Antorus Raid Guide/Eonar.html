Sephuz's Secret

Tips and Tricks

Same as with Portal Keeper Hasabel you will reliably be able to proc your Sephuz's Secret so i suggest speccing into Shadowfury.

Since a lot of the pacs on this fight contain 5-6+ mobs you'll also want to consider playing Alythess's Pyrogenics as you will be casting Rain of Fire quite a lot.
