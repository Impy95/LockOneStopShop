Tips and Tricks

Save your Soul Harvest for the Apocalypse Protocol intermission and pop all your cooldowns on the adds. They take increased damage that decreases throughout the phase until the cast is done.

You can place a Demonic Gateway going from one platform to another, this will allow people to get from platform to platform faster. Be careful with this as you might hit a Diabolic Bomb while traveling in the gateway.
