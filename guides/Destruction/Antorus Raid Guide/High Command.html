Sephuz's Secret

Tips and Tricks

On this fight you want to consider playing Sephuz's Secret as you can proc this by having your imp cast Sear Magic on people affected by Chaos Pulse. I suggest making a mouseover macro for this.

On mythic difficulty you'll want to make sure you have Soul Shards ready to Chaos Bolt the Screaming Shrike's (the bats), if these do not die in time they will eject the person inside the pod.
