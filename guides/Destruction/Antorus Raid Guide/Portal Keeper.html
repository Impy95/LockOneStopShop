Sephuz's Secret

Tips and Tricks

Make sure you cast Incinerate on the target that will make you hit the highest amount of targets since you'll be playing Fire and Brimstone. This way you will gain more Soul Shards, also the imps have to die before they cast.

The reason i suggest you play Shadowfury on this fight is to stun the imps on the platform. Also, this means you should play Sephuz's Secret since you will reliably be able to proc this off cooldown.
