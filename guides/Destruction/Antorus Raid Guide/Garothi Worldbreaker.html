Tips and Tricks

Place your Demonic Circle so you save time running with Decimation, i prefer to place mine in the middle of the room so i can teleport back in

During the Apocalypse Drive intermission, apply your Havoc to either the Decimator or the Annihilator to generate more Soul Shards.

Place a Demonic Gateway going from the back of the room to melee. This will allow people to either stay near the boss dps'ing for longer or get back quickly after Eradication.
