Sephuz's Secret

Tips and Tricks

I suggest playing Sephuz's Secret together with Shadowfury on the Ember of Taeshalach to proc it more or less off cooldown.

You can cast Banish on the Ember of Taeshalach to stop it from moving. This also makes it unable to take damage so it can be very useful. You can break this cc by casting Banish once again.
