Sephuz's Secret

Tips and Tricks

I suggest using Sephuz's Secret because you will be able to use your imps Sear Magic to dispell Sleep Canister from people in your raid. Be careful with this as you want to make sure nobody is standing within 10 yards of the person you're dispelling.

You may want to place a Demonic Gateway going onto the bridge. This will allows your tanks to take it and clear whatever might be on the bridge. This depends on the tactic you're using.
