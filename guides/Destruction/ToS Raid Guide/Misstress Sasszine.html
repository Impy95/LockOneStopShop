Sin'dorei Spite

Depending on how much add damage you're going to be able to do you may want to consider using Alythess's Pyrogenics or Kil'jaeden's Burning Wish.

Tips and Tricks

When there are 3 or more targets up you should cast Rain of Fire instead of Chaos Bolt. If you have Havoc available this goes up to 4 targets. Keep in mind this means all targets are inside the radius of your Rain of Fire for its full duration

Try to use your Demonic Circle to decrease your downtime when dodging Slicing Tornado or Crashing Wave.
