Tips and Tricks

Kil'jaeden is a very long encounter on Mythic. Currently depending on your raids kill time you may be able to get 2 Lord of Flames procs off. Try use this when your raid uses Bloodlust.

This one is going to save your life and your dps a whole lot! Place your Demonic Circle near the middle of the room, this means you can teleport onto your portal when the Rupturing Singularity knocks you back. On mythic this knockback is very far and you will have it up for every single knockback in phase 1 and 2, not counting the intermission phases.

Place your Demonic Gateway in the middle of the room in the beginning of the fight. Then during the 40% shadow intermission you should place it going from Illidan to the bottom right corner of the platform. This will allow people in your raid to avoid one of the Rupturing Singularity knockbacks in this phase. You should click the gateway when there is roughly 1 second left before the singularity explodes.

In the final phase you should place your Demonic Gateway from the middle to the edge of the Rift to the middle of the platform. This will allow people in your raid to dip into the rift and teleport back out when the boss is casting Darkness of a Thousand Souls reducing the overall damage your raid is going to take in this phase.
