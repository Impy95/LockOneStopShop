Sin'dorei Spite

Depending on how much add damage you're going to be able to do you may want to consider using Alythess's Pyrogenics or Kil'jaeden's Burning Wish.

Tips and Tricks

Do your best to cast your Incinerates on the target where you will be able to hit the highest amount of targets when cleaving. This means you will generate more shards and overall do more damage. Also the adds are priority targets so the faster you kill them the better

Place your Demonic Circle off to the side so you can teleport out when the boss starts to draw in pools.
