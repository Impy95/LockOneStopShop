Sin'dorei Spite

Tips and Tricks

On this fight you can help your raid a lot by utilizing your Demonic Circle well. I like to place mine in the back of the room allowing me to teleport out of the way when targeted by a Shadowy Blade. This same teleport can also be used when the boss casts Unbound Chaos again to get out of the way.

Depending on the tactic your raid is using you may not want a lot of unnecessary damage onto the Maiden outside of her shield. This means you should ideally save your Havoc for when she's about to cast this.

In the final phase you may need to re-summon your pet so keep an eye out for that. Also make sure you replace your Demonic Circle and use it to avoid taking fall damage when knocked into the air by Dark Mark.
