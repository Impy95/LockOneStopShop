What does this stat do?

Haste - Haste makes everything faster, cast time, Immolate tick rate, and most importantly reducing the cooldown on conflagrate.

Crit - Incinerate and Immolate both generate more shards from critical strikes. Also your Critical Strike makes your Chaos Bolt hit harder as this spell is a guaranteed crit already.

Versatility - A weaker, but entirely passive, version of mastery.

Mastery - Passively increases your damage and reduces your damage taken, in addition to a random component.

Obviously the precise stat weights are gonna vary a lot depending on your gear, talents and obviously the fight itself. This segment of the guide is just made to give you a general idea of what stats you should be aiming for.

Mastery >= Haste Int > Crit > Vers

Azerite Traits

To view all information about relics please visit the Sims > Azerite page here.

Blizzard is still constantly tuning Azerite Traits. Right now, the standouts are:

  • Crashing Chaos - Gives immense ST burst. Lacks on fights with adds or where you need consistent damage instead of burst damage. Generally only use this trait for ST bosses, or if the boss has a damage increase at some point.
  • Flashpoint - Amazing for situations where theres a target that stays above 80% health or constantly spawning adds that will give you high uptime.
  • Rolling Havoc - Great for situations where you can constantly get value from Havoc.
  • Treacherous Covenant - This trait is extremely powerful while you are above 50% health but comes with a noticeable downside of taking 15% more damage while you are below 20% health. This will be very useful in a raid environment but you have to make sure you are knowledgeable about the fight you are doing to take advantage of this trait. It is not recommended to ever run this in Mythic+.

These traits are servicable and can be picked up now that we have 6 available traits to us

  • Bursting Flare
